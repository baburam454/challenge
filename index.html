Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish perfect for any meal. This classic omelette beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings (choose one or more):

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and peppar untill they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-sticky frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the buffer is melted and building, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (opitional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, them slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
